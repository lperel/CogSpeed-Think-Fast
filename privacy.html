CogSpeed Privacy Policy

CogSpeed Privacy Policy
Effective Date: March 2026 Last Updated: March 2026 Owner: Layne Perelli / Gray Matter Metrics, LLC

1. Overview
CogSpeed is a cognitive performance testing application. This Privacy Policy describes what data CogSpeed collects, how it is stored, and how it is used. We take your privacy seriously and are committed to transparency.

2. Data We Collect
CogSpeed may collect the following information during use:

2a. Profile Information (voluntary)
Email address (used as a subject identifier)
Birth month and year (used to compute age)
Gender
Email results preference

2b. Test Data
Pre-test fatigue rating (Samn-Perelli Fatigue Scale)
Cognitive performance test results (response times, accuracy, CPS score)
Per-trial response log
Test duration and end reason

2c. Location Data (optional, browser-prompted)
Approximate GPS coordinates (latitude/longitude)
Reverse-geocoded address (via OpenStreetMap Nominatim API)
Local and UTC timestamp at time of test
CogSpeed requests location access only for field deployment tracking purposes. Location permission is requested by your browser and you may decline without affecting test functionality.

3. How Data Is Stored
All data is stored locally on your device using browser localStorage. CogSpeed does not transmit your test data, profile, or results to any server operated by Gray Matter Metrics, LLC or any third party, except as described in Section 4.

Data persists on your device until:

You clear your browser's localStorage or site data
A new major version of CogSpeed clears old storage keys on first load

4. Limited Third-Party Data Transmission
If location access is granted, your GPS coordinates are sent to the OpenStreetMap Nominatim API (nominatim.openstreetmap.org) solely to obtain a human-readable address. This is a one-time request per test session. OpenStreetMap's privacy policy governs that interaction: https://osmfoundation.org/wiki/Privacy_Policy

No other third-party services receive your data.

5. Email Results
If you opt in to email results and enter an email address, CogSpeed opens your device's default mail application pre-filled with your results. CogSpeed does not send email directly — no email infrastructure or servers are involved.

6. Data We Do Not Collect
CogSpeed does not collect:

Payment or financial information
Social Security numbers or government IDs
Health records or medical history
Browsing history or cross-site tracking data
Cookies (CogSpeed uses localStorage only)

7. Children
CogSpeed enforces a minimum age of 14 at profile registration. If you are under 14, you may not create a profile or submit test data.

8. Data Retention and Deletion
Because all data is local, you control your own data. To delete all CogSpeed data, clear your browser's site data for the CogSpeed domain, or use your browser's developer tools to clear localStorage.

9. Research and Academic Use
If CogSpeed is deployed in a research or institutional context, the administrator of that deployment is responsible for compliance with applicable data protection laws (including IRB requirements, HIPAA, GDPR, or similar regulations). Gray Matter Metrics, LLC makes no representations regarding regulatory compliance for third-party deployments.

10. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above. Continued use of CogSpeed after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact
For privacy-related inquiries, contact the copyright holder via the GitHub repository: https://github.com/lperel/CogSpeed-Think-Fast
